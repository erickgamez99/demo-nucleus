Summary

  • Revenue is growing but we are 8% below the annual target.
  • Only 62% of class capacity is filled. That means 4 out of every 10 spots sit empty.
  • Member retention is at 74%. The industry average is 80 to 85%. We lose too many people after month 3.
  • Personal training and nutrition coaching are underutilized. Only 11% of members buy add-on services.

Seasonal Patterns

  • January rush: New Year resolutions drive a 35% spike in sign-ups. Most cancel by March.
  • Summer dip: June through August drops 15 to 20% as members go on vacation.
  • Fall rebound: September and October pick back up as people return to routine.
  • Holiday slow: Late November through December drops again. Gift card promos help offset.
